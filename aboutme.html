About Me

Hello! My name is Andrea McDermid Boué, and I am an information professional with a passion for research and literature reviews. I am currently working towards a Master's degree in Information Studies from McGill University, where I specialize in knowledge management, user experience, and information organization.

In my own time, I love cooking and baking, reading, and spending time with family and friends. I especially enjoy exploring Montreal's cafés,libraries, restaurants, and bookstores.
